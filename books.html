Protestant Libertarianism
(Working Title) 		In this book, we take a look at protestant ethics, and how Classical Libertarianism stems from them. It is then compared to other modes of ethics such as Orthodoxy, Catholicism and Ancient Greek schools of philosophy. The purpose of this book is to show why unethical Capitalism should better be called Protestant Libertarianismand, why modern Libertarian countries suffer from Corporatism and crony capitalism, and why Libertarianism requires a strong spiritual and ethical background to achieve mainstream popularity in non-Protestant Western countries.
